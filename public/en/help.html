Help

1. What is Cos4Env?

Cos4Env is a co-designed, interoperable and open source service that collects environmental variables information from multiple citizen observatories in one place, allowing experts to save time in the process of searching for and downloading information on environmental variables and facilitating access to a large amount of information from a single site.

Cos4Env is available on the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) and complies with the FAIR data principles, it is searchable, accessible, interoperable and reusable.

2. Who is behind Cos4Env?

Cos4Env has been developed by Bineo Consulting SL within the European Horizon 2020 Cos4Cloud project, whose main objective is to improve the technologies of citizen observatories to help them increase the quantity and quality of observations and, finally, help guarantee their viability. long-term.

3. How can I register with Cos4Env?

The use of the Cos4Env service does not require user registration since access is made through login in the external service of Authenix. Authenix is part of the European Horizon 2020 Cos4Cloud project, and is available in the European Open Science Cloud EOSC (European Open Science Cloud), you can consult the Privacy Policy of AUthenix and its terms of use for more information.

Although Cos4Env does not require registration, it does give visibility to the contribution made by each expert, allowing them to share their profile with information on the identifications and comments they have made, their searches and downloads.

4. Where are the observations that appear in Cos4Env from?

In Cos4Env you can find records of environmental variables from the citizen observatories integrated into the Cos4Env service, such as OdourCollect or CanAirIO, to facilitate access to information it is possible to apply different search criteria and filters, such as license, type, location, date …

5. Which users is Cos4Env aimed at?

Cos4Env is aimed at expert users or with knowledge of the environment, who want to collaborate by providing information on the records that citizens have published in the different observatories.

6. What actions can be performed in Cos4Env?

Cos4Env is a service that allows experts to consult information on environmental variables from multiple citizen observatories from a single place. From Cos4Env you can:

  • Apply different criteria to filter or search for information such as: type, location, citizen observatory or date.
  • See the details of a record, make comments.
  • See and share my user profile in Cos4Env, comments made, and downloads.
  • Download Citizen Science environmental variables datasets in a standard format.

7. How can I see the detail of an observation?

Entering a registry you can see the file with its details, where the following information appears: registration code, date of publication in the citizen observatory, how many comments it has, name of the citizen observatory from which the observation comes, coordinates, type and value.
